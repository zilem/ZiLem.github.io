STORIES WORTH MAKING

2 features worth looking forward to.

OVER the last 2 years, I've been knee-deep in 2 screenplays based on real-life stories about individuals who derive an extraordinary sense of purpose pursuing social missions involving empowerment. These are prestige dramas that offer blistering social critique to a niche global audience.

For the next 5 years, I shall be collaborating with industry professionals who apart from bringing the films to completion, also believe in the social impact we are making.

The team has already begun to form. If these issues interest you, I look forward to our conversation.
