STORY CONSULTING

Clarify your Vision. Move your Audience.

Everyone has a History.
A History told meaningfully is a Story.
A Story told meaningfully moves an Audience.

AFTER interviewing hundreds across the globe for my films, I'm convinced everyone's got a unique compelling life story. It all comes down to how you tell it.

Here's my method in 2 simple steps:


1.

SELF-DISCOVERY

We'll revisit your early memories, motivations, and emotions. We'll uncover what used to drive you. What used to be meaningful.


Is that still driving you today?
Is that still meaningful to you today?
What do you aspire to stand for today?
Why do you want to stand for that today?


Like a Pathfinder, my forte lies in asking the right questions. Illuminating your blind spots. Getting to the heart of why you are who you are.


2.

ARTICULATION

With the help of screenwriting techniques and concepts illustrated in my blog, we'll articulate your unique strengths in a way that provides value to your audience.

You may even craft your Vision organically as part of your life story. Check out My Story.



Audiences gravitate towards an Authentic, Articulate & Relevant Vision.

YOUR story is not about the Truth. It's about Meaning. It has to be meaningful to you and your audience.
