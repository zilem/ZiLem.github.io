WHO AM I?

Everyone has a History.
History told meaningfully is Story.
Story told meaningfully moves Audience.

If you get this, who better to consult than someone who makes Moving & Inspiring films about Real people? Who has interviewed hundreds around the world. I am convinced everyone's got a unique compelling life story. It comes down to how you tell it.

As Story Consultant for the endeavour to send the first Singaporean to Space, my challenge is to tell variations of the same emotional story to different audiences.

My "Funnel Vision" method begins with self-discovery. Revisiting your early memories, motivations, and emotions. We'll uncover what used to drive you. What used to be meaningful. Then ask...

Is that still driving you today?
Is that still meaningful to you today?
What do you aspire to stand for today?
Why do you aspire to stand for that today?

Like a Pathfinder, my forte lies in asking the right questions. Illuminating your blind spots. Getting to the heart of why you are who you are. Then, I apply screenwriting techniques and z-Axis tools to articulate your Vision.

If you like, you may craft your Vision organically as part of your life story. Check out My Story.

Finally, share your Vision & Story! And...

Witness your audience gravitate towards your authentic concise Vision now.
