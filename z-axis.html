z-Axis.

Out-of-the-plane thinking.

Most experiences in life lead us to think that the solution to a problem is often binary. Either this or that. As if the world exists on a plane.

What if we could elevate ourselves above the plane? How would the problem appear to us? Here is a collection of revelations, values and beliefs that have shaped my path as an artist.


S H A R E            

Respect.

Evaluating deep collaborative relationships.

Between audience & filmmaker. Between filmmaker & collaborators. Between parent & child. It's the cornerstone of any relationship.

But what should you respect someone for?

Character? Knowledge? Seniority? Status? Power? Wealth? Any of these and the relationship will eventually fail. Especially character. Character is a huge red herring. We don't respect all good characters, just as we don't disrespect all bad characters.

Respect someone for what he/she stands for to you. Dissociate from those you don't respect. More importantly, discover why you respect those you associate with. And you discover yourself.


S H A R E            

The One Word you stand for.

I stand for MEANING.

1) What is the One Word for what you want to stand for? In other words, who you aspire to be.

2) What is the One Word for what you think of each significant person around you stands for? Compare that with your One Word. If the two words are polar opposites, the relationship will not work out.

I can't work with people who stand for SHORTCUTS. Not pithy sagely wisdom but the unethical easy way out. In life, there are no shortcuts for the pursuit of deeper meaning.

If you don't know what you stand for, you will be defined by others according to what they choose to see of you. Knowing what you stand for attracts like-minded individuals and helps build your audience.

S H A R E            

Church, Craft & Craving.

A Powerful Paradigm for Artists & Startups.

The 3 vital elements for convincing your audience. CRAFT is the technical knowhow required to accomplish the objective. It is the How?

CRAVING is the hunger beyond passion that drives the journey. It is powered by your unique perspective based on what you stand for. It is the Why?

When an individual other than the Church anoints someone, it does not carry the same symbolic meaning. Because the Church is an authority highly regarded by Christians.

CHURCH refers to an individual or organization highly regarded by your audience that gives you split-second credibility & legitimacy when you can show their endorsement of you.

Stories are meaningful.


S H A R E            

Intersectionality.

In America, the gender discrimination faced by a White Female and the racial discrimination faced by an Asian Male both stem from a toxic white masculine culture.

In Asia & Africa, a self-imposed discrimination aka “Colonial Disease” manifests itself in the local government favouring the White Man.

In Singapore, Malay & Indian minorities struggle to conform under a certain "Chinese privilege" conferred to the Chinese majority, a variant of Sinocentrism experienced in China.

Intersectionality is the Interconnected Nature of Race, Class & Gender that creates overlapping & interdependent systems of Discrimination or Disadvantage.


S H A R E            

Language as a Tool for Thought.

Self-discovery begins with Deconstructing your Intuition.

Most people use language for one purpose. To communicate with others. Consider a scenario where you make a snap judgment based on a first impression that you insist can't be expressed in words.

When you make the effort to verbalise your adaptive unconscious, you gain clarity about who you are. Being cognizant of what drives you, you've now accelerated yourself along the path towards achieving your goals.

Pragmatism as Oppression.

"The most potent weapon of the oppressor is the mind of the oppressed."

- Steven Biko, Anti-Apartheid Activist

What happens when those with the power to say no, lack the balls to say yes to true innovation?

For all that Singapore prides itself on innovation, is it destined to become a 'smart nation' of landlords, materialists & conformists? Pragmatism is the modern day shackles we must break free of. We need role models who champion ideals & autonomy.
