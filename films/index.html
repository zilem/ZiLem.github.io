BASED ON TRUE STORIES

2 features worth looking forward to.

FOR THE PAST two years, I've been knee-deep in two screenplays based on true stories - real-life individuals who derive an extraordinary sense of purpose pursuing compelling causes that empower their communities.

Over the next five years, I'll be collaborating with global professionals, who apart from bringing these films to the big screen, would derive meaning from a social cause they believe in.

The team has already begun to form. If these issues interest you, I look forward to our conversation.
