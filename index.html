Discovering Our Purpose in Life.

In his 1946 seminal book Man’s Search for Meaning, psychologist Viktor E. Frankl writes about identifying a purpose in life as being key to his survival as a concentration camp inmate during WWII.

In today's world, inundated by consumerism, the aspiring individual is in need of identifying a purpose in life if he is to survive the mindless pursuit of materialism as a crutch for his self-esteem.

I discover stories that inspire us when we're in the company of those who boldly pursue a Life of Meaning over a Life of Materialism.
