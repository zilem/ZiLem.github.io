Our Search for Meaning in Life.

Authentic. Cathartic.

Screenwriter William Goldman once said,
“A Hollywood Blockbuster tells you the Lie you Want to Believe. An Indie Film tells you the Truth you Don’t Want to Believe.”

The beauty of True Stories lies in the vagaries of life, where motives are driven by real-life complexities. This allows me to deliver an unadulterated perspective of the morally ambiguous world my characters inhabit.

Issues of Intersectionality.

In America, the gender discrimination faced by a White Female and the racial discrimination faced by an Asian Male both stem from a toxic white masculine culture.

In Asia & Africa, a self-imposed discrimination aka “Colonial Disease” manifests itself in the local government favouring the White Man. Cinema is a great tool to explore these social taboos.

Intersectionality is the Interconnected Nature of Race, Class & Gender that Creates Overlapping & Interdependent Systems of Discrimination or Disadvantage.

Pragmatism as Oppression.

"The most potent weapon of the oppressor is the mind of the oppressed." - Steven Biko

What happens when those with the power to say no, lack the balls to say yes to true innovation?

For all that Singapore prides itself on innovation, is it destined to become a 'smart nation' of landlords, materialists & conformists? I'm interested in true stories about individuals who lead lives of Autonomy & Idealism in the face of pragmatism.

Our Search for Meaning.

Psychologist Viktor E. Frankl details in his seminal book Man’s Search for Meaning his experiences as a WWII concentration camp inmate and his psychotherapeutic method which involved identifying a purpose in life followed by immersively imagining that outcome.

Pragmatism is the modern day shackles we must break free of. We need to cross the chasm from Esteem to Self-Actualization, using Maslow's terms. I discover stories about individuals who make an inquiry about their purpose in our default empty & meaningless life.

Here's to those who consciously pursue a Life of Meaning over a Life of Materialism.
