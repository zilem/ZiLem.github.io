Man's Search for Meaning.

My business is moving & inspiring people with meaning.

THIS site is inspired by psychologist Viktor E. Frankl's 1946 book of the same title. He identifies having a purpose in life as key to his survival as a WWII concentration camp inmate.

Frankl's message resonates deeply for me because the contemporary man seeking self-actualization is identifying a similar purpose in life to survive in a world of mind-numbing materialism.

My purpose in life is the pursuit of Meaning.

MEANING defines my art, my calling & my life's work.

Intro

Artist.  Activist.  Pathfinder.

LEM is an award-winning filmmaker who has written and directed for the last 10 years in the US, UK, China and Singapore. He discovered his vision, his calling and his life’s work after spending years interviewing hundreds across the globe about their life stories. He and his talented international collaborators now develop films about moving and inspiring stories, set in the real world, that explore riveting social issues, making them potential contenders for the foreign language Oscars.

Lem is currently scripting 2 feature films based on the true stories of Asia's largest realistic self-defence movement, and the endeavour to send a man to space in a homemade spacecraft.

Lem studied Art & Architecture in the renowned Cooper Union in NY, Writing & Directing in London and Producing at UCLA. He has worked in the food, finance, consulting and fashion industries, and currently volunteers as a story consultant for the space endeavour. He has lived in 10 countries across 4 continents, and has the uncanny ability to get just about anyone to open up about their life story within minutes, and go on for hours.
