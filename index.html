Why am I obsessed about Meaning?

In his book Man’s Search for Meaning, psychologist Viktor E. Frankl wrote about identifying a purpose in life as being key to his survival as a concentration camp inmate during WWII.

In today's world, the aspiring individual seeking self-actualization is in need of identifying a purpose in life in order to survive the numbing pursuit of materialism, a crutch for his self-esteem.

I stand for a Life of Meaning over a Life of Materialism.
