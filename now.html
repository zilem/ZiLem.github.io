Current Projects

2 feature films that explore humanity & offer social critique.

I'm currently working on the screenplays for two Real-life stories about individuals who derive an extraordinary sense of purpose in their lives. Their larger-than-life personalities are a testament to their ability to Move and Inspire their tribes in Our Search for Meaning. These stories are the perfect for "Smarthouse Films" - prestige dramas that explore humanity, offer blistering social critique and address the needs of a niche global audience.

Of Meek And Menace

Is Martial Arts Self-Defence?

Yew Chye can finish a person in 2 seconds. But he won't. He's been through Hell and back. I went to Los Angeles to co-write Yew Chye’s epic journey from Singapore to Australia to America with long time friend Arlene Bogna.

After surviving childhood abuse and racial discrimination, a single father abandons his corporate career to pursue his calling – founding Asia’s largest self-defense movement to protect and empower women.

You’ll never see the ending coming. This is definitely a film you don't want to miss.

Garage Spaceman

The outlandish true story of a maverick physicist who risks his career, financial ruin and lives, to send the first Singaporean to space in his homemade spacecraft.
