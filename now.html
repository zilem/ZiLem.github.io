Current Projects

2 feature films.

I'm currently working on 2 feature screenplays based on real-life stories about individuals who derive an extraordinary sense of purpose in their lives. Their larger-than-life personalities are a testament to their ability to move and inspire their tribes. These stories are positioned as "Smarthouse Films": prestige dramas that explore humanity, offer blistering social critique and address the needs of a niche global audience.

Of Meek & Menace

Is Martial Arts Self-Defence?

Chye can finish a person in 2 seconds. But he won't. He's been through Hell and back. I went to Los Angeles to co-write his epic journey from Singapore to Australia to America with long time friend Arlene Bogna.

After surviving childhood abuse and racial discrimination, a single father abandons his corporate career to pursue his calling – founding Asia’s largest self-defense movement to protect and empower women.

You’ll never see the ending coming. This is definitely not a film to miss.

Garage Spaceman

The outlandish true story of a maverick physicist who risks his career, financial ruin and lives, to send the first Singaporean to space in his homemade spacecraft.
