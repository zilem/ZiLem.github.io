A wise and influential filmmaker once cautioned me, "To fall in love with filmmaking is life's greatest curse."

At my own peril, I ignored my uncle's advice.

MY LIFE STORY.

An interdisciplinary approach to self-expression.

IN the early 2000s, as a fine arts student, I chanced upon the Asian film Hero. I was enthralled by the use of colour, dance, camera movement, music and stage design. It struck me that Film is the truly interdisciplinary medium I had been seeking to express myself. With that, my journey as a filmmaker began.


S H A R E            

Meaningless.

How my film career took off with a crash.

UPON graduation, I was forced to abandon filmmaking to pull my weight in the family's languishing food business. As a traveling salesman in the US, I survived out of a suitcase, rental car & cheap motels.

I used to think that life's natural progression after school is a fulfilling career. Yet in the following years, I bounced from one firm to the next. Drifting through life without purpose.

Inspiration.

I was searching for an Inspiring Story to be a part of.

ALAS, I gave myself a 6 month break from the corporate machine. I considered returning to filmmaking, but my emotional reservoir was running on empty. I took to the road again. Only this time, I wanted to discover myself.

Absolute freedom guarantees absolute loneliness. 12 years & 10 countries affirmed this truth.

Empathy.

Be authentically Moved and you gain their trust in a heartbeat.

A few years ago, I learnt about a hermit warrior who undergone a crazy transformation after the killing of his brother. He traveled the world to learn true self-defence. He became a teacher and his methods were controversial.

One day, I got Chye to open up about his tragic life story. Not just the war stories that we love bragging about, but the repressed ones we've waited our whole lives to get out. I don't simply connect with people. The art of inhabiting minds evolved out of a necessity to combat loneliness.

Calling.

Through these stories, I found my way Home - a filmmaker who transcends self-expression.

THIS year, I was introduced to yet another phenomenal story. In the annals of Singapore history, it is unimaginable for an individual from this tiny nation state to make an attempt at space travel. No less in his homemade spacecraft. I’m proud to play a part in telling Marvyn's astronomical journey.


My vision as a filmmaker is creating cinema that Move & Inspire us in

Our Search for Meaning.
